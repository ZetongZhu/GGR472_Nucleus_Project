About the Project

Nucleus Independent Living aims to enhance food accessibility in the Halton Region. Our web map visualizes geographic barriers by overlaying data on food banks, residential addresses, and public transit.

Data Sources

  • Canada Census: Dissemination Areas & demographics
  • Halton Region Open Data (Burlington, Milton, Oakville)
  • Public Transit Open Data (e.g., GO Transit)
  • Food Bank Locations provided by partner organizations

Methodology

We combine spatial analysis and interactive web mapping to explore barriers to food access. Key processes include calculating distances using Turf.js, generating buffers and heatmaps, and overlaying multiple datasets.
