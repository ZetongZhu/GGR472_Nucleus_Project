Food Access Mapping – Halton Region

Nucleus Independent Living – GGR472 Final Project

About the Project

This map helps Nucleus Independent Living analyze geographic barriers to food access across the Halton Region.

Data Sources

  • Statistics Canada
  • Halton Region Open Data
  • GO Transit & Local Bus Stops
  • Partner-provided Food Bank Locations

Methodology

Spatial analysis performed with Turf.js hexGrid and distance filtering. Mapbox visualizes service coverage and transit access proximity.
